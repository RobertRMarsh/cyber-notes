## Notes
### Configuration Management 
- process/discipline used to ensure changes made to systems are authorized and validated 
#### Components of Config Management
##### Identification
- Baseline identification of a system: components, interfaces, and documentation
###### Inventory
- the first step in asset management is a list of currently owned information assets/wish list of desired future ones
- difficult to keep track of/ but required to understand what you are protecting
##### (Security) Baselines
- Minimum accepted level of protection
- used as a reference point 
###### Baselines
- total inventory of all the systems: component's, data, hard/software, admin controls
- helpful in achieving minimum levels 
##### Change Control
- update process for requesting changes to the baseline
- review and approval should be required for all changes
- including updates/patches to systems
##### Verification & Audit
- check if anything was broken by new changes
- audit process checks if the new baseline matches and exceeds old baseline in the desired areas
