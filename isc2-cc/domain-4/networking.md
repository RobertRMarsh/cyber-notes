## Notes
### Networks at a glance
- in home networks, firewalls, routers, and switches are usually combined into one device
- in small business networks a switch sits behind a firewall, and the switch connects all the internal devices
- additionally the firewall is behind the router
- 2<=computers linked together to share; data, resources, and info
#### Types of Networks
##### LAN(Local Area Network)
- typically spanning a single building/floor 
###### Invading a LAN
- need to be close by, 
- sniffer taps onto cables
- plugging in USB devices
- using other tools that need physical access
##### WAN(Wide Area Network)
- long distance connection between geographically remote networks
#### WIFI
- wireless networking
- comes with additional vulnerabilities
- intrusions can happen at a distance
#### Ethernet
- IEEE 802.3
	- standard that defines wired connections of networked devices
	- defines how data is formatted over the wire
