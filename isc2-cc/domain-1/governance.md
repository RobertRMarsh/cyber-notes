## Notes
### Governance Hierarchy
- Procedures support Policies
- Policies support Standards
- Standards adhere to Regulations/Laws
### Each level explained
- Regulations/Laws are issued (typically) by government and come with fines 
- Standards immerge from regulations/laws and are used by governance teams to make frameworks for policies & procedures that support adherence to regulations
- Policies are put in place by org governance (i.e. executive management) 
- Procedures are the detailed steps to complete a task that supports org policies.
### Business/Legal Agreements
#### SLA(Service Level Agreement)
- defines minimum acceptable service from a cloud provider
- granular details
#### MOU/MOA/JOA 
- competitors share resources in emergency
- what can be done with system, less granular detail
