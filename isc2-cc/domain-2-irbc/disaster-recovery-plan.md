## Notes
### DRP (Disaster Recovery Plan)
- guides emergency personnel until business is restored to last full-known reliable operations
- restores IT and comms needed by the org 
#### Components of an DRP:
- Executive summary providing a high-level overview of the plan  
- Technical guides for IT personnel responsible for implementing and  maintaining critical backup systems
- Full copies of the plan for critical disaster recovery team members
- Critical disaster recovery team members will have checklists to help guide their actions amid a disaster.  
- IT personnel will have technical guides helping them get the alternate sites up and running. 
- Managers and public relations personnel will have simple-to-follow, high-level documents to help them communicate the issue accurately without requiring input from team members who are busy working on the recovery.  
- Department-specific plans, checklists for certain individuals
### BCP VS DRP
- BCP is about maintaining critical business functions
- DRP is about restoring IT and Comms
